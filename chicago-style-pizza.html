Best Chicago-style Pizza Places in Chicagoland Area

Nancy’s Pizza

208 S Weber Rd.
Bolingbrook, IL 60440
(630) 679-0700
Visit Website

Nancy's Pizza is known for its deep-dish pizza, a Chicago specialty. Their pizza features a thick, buttery crust, topped with a generous amount of cheese, and savory tomato sauce. It's a must-visit if you're a fan of classic Chicago-style deep-dish pizza.

Rosati’s Pizza

329 N Schmidt Rd.
Bolingbrook, IL 60440
(630) 759-6060
Visit Website

Rosati's is a well-loved local chain known for its delicious thin-crust and pan-style pizzas. They offer a variety of toppings and specialty pizzas, providing options for everyone's taste buds.

Fat Ricky’s

660 N Independence Blvd.
Romeoville, IL 60446
(815) 293-2900
Visit Website

Fat Ricky's is known for its variety of pizza styles, including thin-crust, deep-dish, and stuffed pizzas. They are praised for their generous portions and delicious Italian-American fare.

Lou Malnati’s Pizzeria

1038 Lake St.
Oak Park, IL 60301
(708) 358-0800
Visit Website

Lou Malnati's is renowned for its classic Chicago-style deep-dish pizza, featuring a buttery crust, ample cheese, and a flavorful tomato sauce. It's a favorite for those seeking an authentic deep-dish experience.

Giordano’s

1115 Chicago Ave
Oak Park, IL 60302
(708) 386-2223
Visit Website

Giordano's is famous for its stuffed deep-dish pizza, featuring layers of cheese, toppings, and a buttery crust. It's a beloved choice for those who crave a hearty, cheesy pizza experience.

Italian Fiesta Pizzeria

8058 S Halsted St.
Chicago, IL 60620
(773) 684-2222
Visit Website

Italian Fiesta is known for its tasty and affordable thin-crust pizza with a variety of toppings. They have multiple locations in Chicago, making it a convenient choice for locals and visitors alike.

Art of Pizza

3033 N Ashland Ave.
Chicago, IL 60657
(773) 327-5600
Visit Website

Art of Pizza offers an array of gourmet toppings and specialty pizzas. They are known for their creative flavor combinations and commitment to using high-quality ingredients.
